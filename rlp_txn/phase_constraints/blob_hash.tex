We use the following shorthands:
\[
    \left\{ \begin{array}{lcl}
        \locBlobHashLengthCountdown        _{i} & \define & \rlpTxnComputationColumnAuxiliaryData{1}                   _{i} \\
        \locBlobHashIndex                  _{i} & \define & \rlpTxnComputationColumnCounterConstantAuxiliaryData{1}    _{i} \\
    \end{array}                            \right.
\]

We first determine the \rlp{} prefix of the data on the first row:
\begin{enumerate}
        \item \If $\isTxn _{i - 1} = 1$ \Then:
        \[
            \rlpProcessBlobHash {
                anchorRow        = i                                  ,
                relOffset        = 0                                  ,
                byteStringLength = \locBlobHashLengthCountdown _{i}   ,
                firstByte        = \nothing                           ,
                isList           = \true                              ,
                mustBeNontrivial = \true                              ,
            }
        \] 
\end{enumerate}

And propagate the phase size counter:
\begin{enumerate}[resume]
    \item \If $\isCmp _{i} = 1$ \Then $\locBlobHashLengthCountdown_{i} = \locBlobHashLengthCountdown_{i - 1} - \lc_{i-1} \cdot \rlpTxnComputationColumnLimbSize_{i-1} $
\end{enumerate}

And we RLP-ize each blob hash:
\begin{enumerate}[resume]
    \item \If $\isTxn _{i - 1} = 0$ \et $\done _{i - 1} = 1$ \Then:
    \[
            \rlpProcessBytesThirtyTwo {
                anchorRow = i                    ,
                relOffset = 0                    ,
                dataHi    = \loc{blob\_hash\_hi} ,
                dataLo    = \loc{blob\_hash\_lo} ,
            }
        \]
        where
        \[
            \left\{ \begin{array}{lcl}
                \loc{blob\_hash\_hi} & \define & \rlpTxnComputationColumnExoDataColumn{1} _{i} \\
                \loc{blob\_hash\_lo} & \define & \rlpTxnComputationColumnExoDataColumn{2} _{i} \\
            \end{array} \right.
        \]
\end{enumerate}

And we define the index of the blob hash:
\begin{enumerate}[resume]
    \item $\locBlobHashIndex _{i} = \isCmp _{i} \cdot \isCmp _{i - 1} \cdot (\locBlobHashIndex _{i - 1} + \done _{i - 1})$
\end{enumerate}

Finalization constraint:
\begin{enumerate}[resume]
    \item \If $\locBlobHashLengthCountdown _{i} =    0$ \Then $\phaseEnd _{i} =  1$
    \item \If $\locBlobHashLengthCountdown _{i} \neq 0$ \Then $\phaseEnd _{i} =  0$ 
\end{enumerate}
