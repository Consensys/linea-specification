\begin{center}
	\boxed{\text{All constraints in this section assume that
	\(
	\left\{ \begin{array}{lcl}
		\totalTransactionNumber _{i} & \neq & \totalTransactionNumber _{i - 1} \\
		\user                   _{i} & =    & 1                                \\
		\txSkip                 _{i} & =    & 1                                \\
	\end{array} \right.
	\)
	}}
\end{center}
In other words: row $i$ marks the beginning of the processing of a new transaction which (supposedly) requires no \textsc{evm}-execution.
We impose the views over the next rows:
\begin{enumerate}
	\item The following rows (in which we include the present row with index $i$) peek into account data and finally peek into transaction data:
	\[
		\left[ \begin{array}{cl}
			+ & \peekTransaction _{i + \roffTxSkipUserTransaction                  } \\
			+ & \peekAccount     _{i + \roffTxSkipUserSenderAccount                } \\
			+ & \peekAccount     _{i + \roffTxSkipUserRecipientAccount             } \\
			+ & \peekAccount     _{i + \roffTxSkipUserRecipientOrDelegationAccount } \\
			+ & \peekAccount     _{i + \roffTxSkipUserCoinbaseAccount              } \\
			+ & \peekContext     _{i + \roffTxSkipUserZeroContext                  } \\
		\end{array} \right]
		= 
		\nsrTxSkipUser
	\]
\end{enumerate}
We provide a brief summary of what these rows represent
\begin{description}
	\item[\underline{Transaction-row $n^\circ \; \roffTxSkipUserTransaction                  $:}]
		we load the transaction;
	\item[\underline{Account-row     $n^\circ \; \roffTxSkipUserSenderAccount                $:}]
		we load the sender and deduct the value and gas cost of the transaction from its balance;
	\item[\underline{Account-row     $n^\circ \; \roffTxSkipUserRecipientAccount             $:}]
		we load the recipient and add the value to its balance;
	\item[\underline{Account-row     $n^\circ \; \roffTxSkipUserRecipientOrDelegationAccount $:}]
		if the recipient is delegated we load the delegate, otherwise we re-load the recipient again;
		in either case we don't modify the account, we simply read it;
	\item[\underline{Account-row     $n^\circ \; \roffTxSkipUserCoinbaseAccount              $:}]
		we load the coinbase address and pay it;
	\item[\underline{Context-row     $n^\circ \; \roffTxSkipUserZeroContext                  $:}]
\end{description}
