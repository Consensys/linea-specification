We define the remaining context initialization parameterns,
i.e. those marked with $(\textcolor{draculaorange}{\bigstar})$
in section~(\ref{hub: instruction handling: call: specialized: initializing callee}),
as follows:
\[
	\locChildContextIsStatic
	\define
	\left[ \begin{array}{clcl}
		+ & \locIsCall         & \cdot & \locCurrentContextIsStatic \\
		+ & \locIsCallcode     & \cdot & \locCurrentContextIsStatic \\
		+ & \locIsDelegatecall & \cdot & \locCurrentContextIsStatic \\
		+ & \locIsStaticcall   & \cdot & \one         \\
	\end{array} \right] 
\]
and
\[
	\left\{ \begin{array}{lcl}
		\locChildContextAccountAddressHi & \define &
		\left[ \begin{array}{clcl}
			+ & \locIsCall         & \cdot & \locCalleeAddressHi         \\
			+ & \locIsCallcode     & \cdot & \locCurrentAccountAddressHi \\
			+ & \locIsDelegatecall & \cdot & \locCurrentAccountAddressHi \\
			+ & \locIsStaticcall   & \cdot & \locCalleeAddressHi         \\
		\end{array} \right] \vspace{2mm} \\
		\locChildContextAccountAddressLo & \define &
		\left[ \begin{array}{clcl}
			+ & \locIsCall         & \cdot & \locCalleeAddressLo         \\
			+ & \locIsCallcode     & \cdot & \locCurrentAccountAddressLo \\
			+ & \locIsDelegatecall & \cdot & \locCurrentAccountAddressLo \\
			+ & \locIsStaticcall   & \cdot & \locCalleeAddressLo         \\
		\end{array} \right] \\
	\end{array} \right.
\]
and
\[
	\hspace*{-1.5cm}
	\locChildContextAccountDeploymentNumber \define
	\left[ \begin{array}{clcl}

and
\[
