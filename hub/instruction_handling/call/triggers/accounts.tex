The following shorthand is used to determine when to load the contents of the \callerName{} account.
This happens precisely when the \inst{CALL}-type instruction is unexceptional.
As such we impose
\[
	\left\{ \begin{array}{lcl}
		\locCallRequiresCalleeAccount  & \define & \miscStpFlag                    _{i + \callMiscRowOffset          } \\
		\locCallRequiresCallerAccount  & \define & \scenCallUnexceptional          _{i + \callFirstScenarioRowOffset } \\
		\locCallRequiresAccountsTwice  & \define & \scenCallRequiresAccountsTwice  _{i + \callFirstScenarioRowOffset } \\
		\locCallRequiresAccountsThrice & \define & \scenCallRequiresAccountsThrice _{i + \callFirstScenarioRowOffset } \\
	\end{array} \right.
\]
