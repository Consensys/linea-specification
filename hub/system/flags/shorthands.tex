We define the following shorthands
\[
        \left\{ \begin{array}{lcr}
                \flagSum _{i} & \define &
                \left[ \begin{array}{cl}
                        + & \sysi _{i} \\
                        + & \user _{i} \\
                        + & \sysf _{i} \\
                \end{array} \right]
                \vspace{2mm}
                \\
                \weightedFlagSum _{i} & \define &
                \left[ \begin{array}{crcl}
                        + & 2^{0} & \!\!\! \cdot \!\!\! & \sysi _{i} \\
                        + & 2^{1} & \!\!\! \cdot \!\!\! & \user _{i} \\
                        + & 2^{2} & \!\!\! \cdot \!\!\! & \sysf _{i} \\
                \end{array} \right]
        \end{array} \right.
\]
