We define constraints pertaining to the $\accRomLexFlag$
\[
	\accConditionallyTriggerRomLex {
		anchorRow = i ,
		relOffset = \relof ,
		condition = \loc{condition} ,
	}
	\define \accRomLexFlag _{i + \relof} = \loc{condition}
\]
and in particular
\[
	\left\{ \begin{array}{lcl}
		\accDontTriggerRomLex {
			anchorRow = i ,
			relOffset = \relof ,
		}
		& \define & \accRomLexFlag _{i + \relof} = 0
		\vspace{2mm} \\
		\accDoTriggerRomLex {
			anchorRow = i ,
			relOffset = \relof ,
		}
		& \define & \accRomLexFlag _{i + \relof} = 1
		\\
	\end{array} \right.
\]
