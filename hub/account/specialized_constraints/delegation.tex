Below $\relof$ is a (small) positive integer.
We introduce constraints pertaining to account delegation.
\[
	\left\{ \begin{array}{lcl}
		\accSetDelegationAddress {
			anchorRow           = i                             ,
			relOffset           = \relof                        ,
			delegationAddressHi = \loc{address\_hi} ,
			delegationAddressLo = \loc{address\_lo} ,
		}
		\vspace{2mm}
		\\
		\qquad \define
		\left\{ \begin{array}{lcl}
			\accDelegationAddressNewHi _{i + \relof} & = & \loc{address\_hi} \\
			\accDelegationAddressNewLo _{i + \relof} & = & \loc{address\_lo} \\
		\end{array} \right.
		\vspace{2mm}
		\\
		\accSameDelegationAddress {
			anchorRow           = i                             ,
			relOffset           = \relof                        ,
		}
		\vspace{2mm}
		\\
		\qquad \define
		\left\{ \begin{array}{lcl}
			\accDelegationAddressNewHi _{i + \relof} & = & \accDelegationAddressHi _{i + \relof} \\
			\accDelegationAddressNewLo _{i + \relof} & = & \accDelegationAddressLo _{i + \relof} \\
		\end{array} \right.
	\end{array} \right.
\]
