The purpose of the present lookup argument is to delegate the proof of the lower bond of \blockHashArgument{} to the \wcpMod{} module.
The lookup is structured as follows:
\begin{description}
	\item[\underline{Selector:}] $\isPreprocessing$:
	\item[\underline{Source columns:}] ---
		\begin{multicols}{3}
			\begin{enumerate}
				\item $\argOneHi$
				\item $\argOneLo$
				\item $\argTwoHi$
				\item $\argTwoLo$
				\item $\res$
				\item $\instruction$
			\end{enumerate}
		\end{multicols}
	\item[\underline{Target columns:}] ---
		\begin{multicols}{3}
		\begin{enumerate}
			\item $\argOneHi$
			\item $\argOneLo$
			\item $\argTwoHi$
			\item $\argTwoLo$
			\item $\resLo$
			\item $\INST$
		\end{enumerate}
		\end{multicols}
\end{description}
